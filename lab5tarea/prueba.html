User Registration & Application Process

For Contractors

  • Fill out a simple registration form with your basic information
  • Verify your email address to activate your account
  • Create a company profile (optional for enhanced visibility)
  • Browse worker categories and post jobs immediately

For Workers

  • Register with your basic information and verify your email
  • Submit required documentation: government-issued ID, proof of address
  • Provide evidence of qualifications or portfolio of previous work
  • Pay registration fee and wait for application approval

Categorization System

How Workers Are Categorized

Our system organizes workers into skill-based categories, allowing contractors to easily find the right talent for their specific needs. Categories become fully active once they have at least 5 registered workers.

Category Activation Thresholds:

  • Pending: 1-4 registered workers
  • Active: 5+ registered workers

Benefits of Categorization:

  • Easier discovery for contractors seeking specific skills
  • Fair competition among workers with similar skills
  • Specialized rating systems tailored to each profession

Rating and Review System

How Ratings Work

After job completion, contractors can rate workers based on:

  • Quality of work (1-5 stars)
  • Timeliness (1-5 stars)
  • Communication (1-5 stars)
  • Value for money (1-5 stars)

The overall rating is calculated as an average of these four criteria.

Workers with higher ratings appear higher in search results and category listings.

Impact on Recommendations

Featured in "Top Rated" section
Priority in search results
Standard visibility
Lower placement in listings

Workers can improve their ratings by providing excellent service, addressing feedback, and maintaining high professional standards.
